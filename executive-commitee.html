Our Executive Commitee

The Executive Commitee or ec is consist of Professors, Student and other IIT Members, it consists of 9 members, 3 of each type to consider everyones prespective for the betterment of Shiksha Sopan education level which has various work ranging from budget alloacation and organising schoolrship examination and summer camps, the elections for ec are held every three years and ec is selected by the democratic by the means of open voting or closed if required.
